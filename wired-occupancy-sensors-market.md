<p><p>Wired occupancy sensors are categorized by technology and coverage area. Technologically, they include Ultrasonic sensors that use sound waves, Infrared (IR) sensors that detect heat signatures, and combinations like Ultrasonic + Passive Infrared (PIR) for enhanced accuracy. Coverage areas vary, with options for Less than 89°, 90–179°, and 180–360° to suit different environments. The Ultrasonic + PIR type leverages both sound and heat detection, offering a comprehensive solution for maximizing occupancy detection in various settings.</p></p>
