<p><p>The medical imaging instrumentation market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **X-ray systems** utilize radiation to produce images of the body's internal structures. </p><p>2. **Ultrasound devices** employ high-frequency sound waves for real-time imaging, often used in prenatal care. </p><p>3. **MRI** (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) provides detailed images using magnetic fields and radio waves, ideal for soft tissue analysis. </p><p>4. **Computed tomography (CT)** combines X-ray images taken from various angles for cross-sectional views. </p><p>5. **Nuclear imaging** uses radiopharmaceuticals to visualize metabolic processes. </p><p>6. **Others** include systems like fluoroscopy and photoacoustic imaging.</p></p>
