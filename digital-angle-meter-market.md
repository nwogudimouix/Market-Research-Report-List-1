<p><strong>The Digital Angle Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Method</li><li>Electrolyte Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital angle meter market primarily features two main types: the MEMS method and the electrolyte method. The MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) method utilizes micro-sensors to deliver precise angular measurements through inertial sensors. In contrast, the electrolyte method involves the use of conductive fluids to determine angles based on electrical properties, providing reliable measurements for various applications. Both methods are designed to enhance accuracy and ease of use in measuring angles in industrial, construction, and technical fields.</p></p>
