<p><strong>The Photoresist Anti-reflective Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Anti-reflective Coatings</li><li>Top Anti-reflective Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photoresist Anti-reflective Coatings (ARC) market is categorized into Bottom Anti-reflective Coatings (BARCs) and Top Anti-reflective Coatings (TARCs). BARCs are applied beneath the photoresist layer to minimize reflections from the substrate, enhancing pattern fidelity during lithography. TARCs, on the other hand, are placed on top of the photoresist to reduce light reflection from the air, improving imaging quality. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing to achieve high-resolution patterns and improve overall device performance.</p></p>
