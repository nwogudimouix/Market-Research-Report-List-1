<p><p>Transcatheter Heart Valve Replacement and Repair encompasses minimally invasive procedures for treating heart valve diseases. TAVR (Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement) primarily addresses aortic stenosis via catheter insertion, allowing valve delivery without open surgery. TPVR (Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve Replacement) targets pulmonary valve issues, enhancing blood flow and alleviating pressure. TMVR (Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair/Replacement) focuses on mitral valve conditions, improving function through either repair or replacement. Each type represents significant advancements in cardiac care, minimizing recovery times and surgical risks.</p></p>
