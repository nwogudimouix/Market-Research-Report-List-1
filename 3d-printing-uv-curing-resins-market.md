<p><ul><li>Free Radical Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printing UV-curing resins market comprises primarily two types: free radical and cationic resins. Free radical resins utilize photoinitiators to create free radicals when exposed to UV light, initiating polymerization and forming solid structures quickly. Conversely, cationic resins rely on cationic polymerization, where chemical reactions are triggered by UV light, resulting in different curing characteristics and greater resistance to heat and solvents. These distinct types cater to various applications, influencing production speed and material properties.</p></p>
