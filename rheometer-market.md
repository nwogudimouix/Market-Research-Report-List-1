<p><p>The rheometer market comprises various types, primarily including dynamic rheometers, capillary rheometers, and torque rheometers. Dynamic rheometers measure the viscous and elastic properties of materials under controlled temperature and shear conditions, making them suitable for behaviors in dynamic flow. Capillary rheometers assess the flow properties of materials through narrow gaps under pressure, ideal for polymers. Torque rheometers evaluate the torque response of materials during mixing and processing, essential in polymer and food industries. Other types encompass specialized instruments catering to niche applications.</p></p>
