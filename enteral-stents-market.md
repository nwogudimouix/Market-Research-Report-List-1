<p><strong>The Enteral Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Esophageal Stents</li><li>Gastroduodenal Stents</li><li>Colon Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enteral stents market is categorized into esophageal stents, gastroduodenal stents, and colon stents. Esophageal stents are designed to manage strictures and obstructive lesions in the esophagus, improving swallowing and nutrition. Gastroduodenal stents facilitate the passage of food from the stomach to the duodenum, commonly used for gastric outlet obstruction. Colon stents are utilized to treat colorectal obstructions, allowing for bowel function restoration. Each type addresses specific gastrointestinal challenges, contributing to enhanced patient care and quality of life.</p></p>
