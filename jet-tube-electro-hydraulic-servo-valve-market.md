<p><p>The Jet Tube Electro-hydraulic Servo Valve market includes two primary types: two-stage and three-stage servo valves. Two-stage servo valves consist of a pilot stage that controls a larger output stage, offering simple design and robust performance for moderate flow applications. In contrast, three-stage servo valves introduce an additional stage for enhanced precision and control, making them suitable for complex systems requiring fine adjustments. Both types are integral to various hydraulic systems, providing efficient and responsive control in industrial and aerospace applications.</p></p>
