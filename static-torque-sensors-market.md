<p><strong>The Static Torque Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Torque Sensors</li><li>Reaction Torque Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The static torque sensors market primarily consists of two types: rotary torque sensors and reaction torque sensors. Rotary torque sensors measure torque directly in rotating applications, capturing data while the shaft is in motion, making them ideal for motors and generators. Reaction torque sensors, on the other hand, measure torque by detecting the reaction forces at rest, typically used in dynamic testing and assembly applications. Both types are crucial for ensuring precision and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
