<p><p>Heat press machines for garment printing come in three primary types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require the operator to manually press the plates together, making them cost-effective but labor-intensive. Semi-automatic machines automate some processes, offering a blend of efficiency and user input, suitable for small to medium-scale operations. Fully automatic machines are designed for high-volume production, requiring minimal operator intervention and ensuring consistent quality, ideal for large-scale garment businesses looking for efficiency and speed.</p></p>
