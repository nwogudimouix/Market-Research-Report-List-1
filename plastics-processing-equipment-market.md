<p><p>The plastics processing equipment market encompasses various techniques essential for shaping and molding plastic materials. Injection molding involves forcing molten plastic into a mold to create precise shapes. Extrusion continuously produces items like pipes and sheets by pushing plastic through a die. Blow molding forms hollow objects by inflating a heated plastic tube. Thermoforming uses heat to soften plastic sheets, which are then shaped in molds. Other methods include rotational molding and compression molding, catering to diverse applications and product requirements.</p></p>
