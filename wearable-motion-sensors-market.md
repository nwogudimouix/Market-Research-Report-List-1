<p><p>The wearable motion sensors market comprises several key types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetic sensors, and others. Accelerometers detect changes in motion and orientation, primarily measuring acceleration. Gyroscopes measure rotational movement, providing insights into angular velocity. Magnetic sensors, often utilized for compass applications, detect magnetic fields to determine orientation relative to Earth's magnetic north. The "others" category includes various specialized sensors like pressure sensors and environmental sensors, enhancing the functionality of wearables across health, fitness, and smart technology sectors.</p></p>
