<p><strong>The Flat-Field Scanning Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>F-theta Scanning Lenses</li><li>Telecentric Scanning Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flat-Field Scanning Lens market comprises primarily two types: F-theta scanning lenses and telecentric scanning lenses. F-theta scanning lenses are designed to provide a flat field of view, ensuring consistent image quality across the entire field, making them ideal for laser marking and engraving applications. Telecentric scanning lenses maintain a constant magnification and eliminate perspective distortion, which is crucial for precision measurements and quality control. Both types play vital roles in industrial processes that require accuracy and high-resolution imaging.</p></p>
