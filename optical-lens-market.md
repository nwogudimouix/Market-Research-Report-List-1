<p><p>The optical lens market is dominated by two primary types: resin lenses and optical glass lenses. Resin lenses, made from lightweight synthetic materials, offer advantages such as impact resistance, ease of manufacturing, and various tint options. They are commonly used in eyewear and optical devices. On the other hand, optical glass lenses are known for superior optical clarity and scratch resistance, making them preferable for high-precision applications like cameras and microscopes. Each type serves distinct consumer needs and industry applications.</p></p>
