<p><strong>The Copolyester Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Copolyester Resin</li><li>Thermosetting Copolyester Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copolyester resins market is divided into thermoplastic and thermosetting types. Thermoplastic copolyester resins are characterized by their ability to be reshaped upon heating, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility and durability, such as automotive parts and consumer goods. In contrast, thermosetting copolyester resins harden permanently when cured, offering enhanced chemical resistance and stability, which are ideal for applications in coatings, adhesives, and electrical components. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by their unique properties and functionalities.</p></p>
