<p><strong>The Camera Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NiMH Batteries</li><li>Lithium Ion Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera battery market primarily features two types: Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) and Lithium-Ion (Li-ion) batteries. NiMH batteries are known for their environmental friendliness and moderate energy density, making them suitable for basic models and entry-level cameras. In contrast, Lithium-Ion batteries are favored for their higher energy density, longer life cycles, and lighter weight, making them ideal for professional and advanced cameras. The choice between these batteries affects camera performance, recharge efficiency, and overall user experience.</p></p>
