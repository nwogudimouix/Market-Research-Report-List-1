<p><p>The passive oscilloscope probe market is categorized into various types based on their attenuation factors. The 1× probes deliver a direct, unattenuated signal to the oscilloscope, ideal for low-frequency applications. The 10× probes, commonly used, reduce the signal amplitude while increasing bandwidth, making them suitable for general-purpose measurements. The 100× probes provide significant attenuation for high-frequency signals, allowing for precise observations without signal distortion. Other variants may include specialty probes designed for specific applications, such as high-voltage or differential measurements.</p></p>
