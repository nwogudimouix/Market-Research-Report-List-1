<p><strong>The Sliding Door Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Damper</li><li>Pneumatic Damper</li><li>Hydraulic Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sliding door damper market is categorized into three main types: mechanical dampers, pneumatic dampers, and hydraulic dampers. Mechanical dampers utilize springs and friction to control motion, ensuring smooth sliding door operation. Pneumatic dampers leverage compressed air to regulate speed and force, offering precision in performance. Hydraulic dampers use fluid dynamics to provide resistance and control movement effectively, often found in heavy-duty applications. Each type is designed to enhance door functionality, safety, and stability in various settings.</p></p>
