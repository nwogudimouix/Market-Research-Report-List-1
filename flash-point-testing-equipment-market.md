<p><strong>The Flash Point Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cup Flash Point Tester</li><li>Closed Cup Flash Point Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flash Point Testing Equipment market consists of two main types: Open Cup Flash Point Testers and Closed Cup Flash Point Testers. Open Cup testers involve exposing a liquid to an open atmosphere, allowing for ambient air interaction, suitable for measuring higher flash points. Closed Cup testers, on the other hand, contain the sample in a sealed environment, minimizing evaporation and providing more accurate measurements for lower flash points. Both types are essential for assessing the flammability and safety of various substances.</p></p>
