<p><p>The Auto Chassis Dyno market comprises two main types: Single Roller and Multi Roller types. Single Roller Dynos utilize a single roller to simulate load on the vehicle, making them suitable for straightforward performance testing of lighter vehicles. In contrast, Multi Roller Dynos incorporate multiple rollers, allowing for a more comprehensive assessment of heavier vehicles and complex performance metrics. This type offers enhanced accuracy in measuring power, torque, and overall vehicle dynamics, catering to a broader range of application needs.</p></p>
