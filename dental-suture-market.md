<p><strong>The Dental Suture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental suture market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable sutures gradually break down in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal, making them ideal for soft tissue applications such as gums. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are made from materials that resist degradation, requiring eventual removal and suitable for more permanent applications. Each type serves distinct surgical needs, affecting their usage in dental procedures based on healing requirements and patient care.</p></p>
