<p><p>The home blood pressure monitor market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Digital Blood Pressure Monitors**: These devices use electronic sensors to measure blood pressure and provide digital readings, often with additional features like memory storage and Bluetooth connectivity. </p><p>2. **Mercury Blood Pressure Monitors**: Although less common due to safety concerns, these traditional devices use mercury in a glass column to measure pressure accurately.</p><p>3. **Aneroid Blood Pressure Monitors**: Manual devices with a dial gauge, these monitors require a stethoscope for reading and are portable, often used in both home and clinical settings.</p></p>
