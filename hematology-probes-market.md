<p><p>The hematology probes market is categorized into several types, including chromosome enumeration probes, microdeletion probes, and preimplantation probes. Chromosome enumeration probes are designed to assess chromosome number abnormalities for conditions like Down syndrome. Microdeletion probes detect small chromosomal deletions linked to genetic disorders. Preimplantation probes are utilized in assisted reproductive technologies to screen embryos for genetic issues before implantation. Together, these probes enhance diagnostic accuracy and support personalized medicine in hematology and reproductive health.</p></p>
