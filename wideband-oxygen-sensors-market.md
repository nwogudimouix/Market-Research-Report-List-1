<p><strong>The Wideband Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Battery Type</li><li>Critical Current Type</li><li>Pump Battery Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wideband oxygen sensors market encompasses three primary types: battery type, critical current type, and pump battery type. Battery type sensors utilize a built-in power source for operation, offering flexibility in design and installation. Critical current type sensors rely on measuring the oxygen concentration by analyzing the current flow, providing high accuracy. Pump battery type sensors incorporate a pump mechanism that actively controls gas flow, enhancing measurement precision. Together, these types cater to various automotive and industrial applications, optimizing emissions control and engine efficiency.</p></p>
