<p><p>Art database software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software allows users to access their databases remotely via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates, making it ideal for galleries and artists who need real-time collaboration. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on individual computers or servers, providing more control over data security and customization but requiring significant hardware investments and maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
