<p><ul><li>Bias Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber solid tires market primarily consists of two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric cords set at angles to the direction of travel, providing flexibility and durability for various applications. They are often utilized in industrial vehicles and off-road equipment. Radial tires, on the other hand, have cords running perpendicular to the direction of travel, enhancing fuel efficiency, traction, and performance. These tires are commonly found in consumer and commercial vehicles, offering improved ride quality and longevity.</p></p>
