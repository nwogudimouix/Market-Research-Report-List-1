<p><strong>The CO2 Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical CO2 Sensors</li><li>NDIR CO2 Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 sensors market is primarily categorized into two main types: chemical CO2 sensors and non-dispersive infrared (NDIR) CO2 sensors. Chemical CO2 sensors detect carbon dioxide through chemical reactions, often yielding faster results but may require regular maintenance and calibration. In contrast, NDIR CO2 sensors utilize infrared light absorption to measure CO2 concentration, providing high accuracy and stability over time, making them suitable for various applications, including indoor air quality monitoring and industrial processes. Both types play crucial roles in environmental monitoring and safety.</p></p>
