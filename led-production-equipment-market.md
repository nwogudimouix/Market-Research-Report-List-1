<p><p>The LED production equipment market comprises various specialized tools essential for manufacturing LEDs. MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment is used to create thin films for LED substrates. Lithography equipment patterns circuits on semiconductor wafers, while dry etch equipment removes material to define structures. PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment deposits films at low temperatures. PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment coats surfaces for reflectivity and conductivity. Back-end LED production equipment focuses on packaging and assembly processes, ensuring device functionality.</p></p>
