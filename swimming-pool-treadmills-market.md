<p><strong>The Swimming Pool Treadmills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Treadmills</li><li>Electrodynamic Treadmills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swimming pool treadmills market features two main types: mechanical and electrodynamic. Mechanical treadmills operate using a belt driven by the swimmer's movement, offering a straightforward design and minimal maintenance. They provide a controlled resistance environment, ideal for rehabilitation and fitness. In contrast, electrodynamic treadmills utilize electrical components to create adjustable resistance and speed settings, enhancing the user experience. These treadmills often include advanced features like real-time performance tracking, catering to both fitness enthusiasts and rehabilitation programs.</p></p>
