<p><p>The cooking vegetable oil market encompasses various types, each with distinct characteristics. Coconut oil, extracted from coconuts, offers unique flavor and health benefits due to its medium-chain fatty acids. Soybean oil, a common choice in food processing, is rich in polyunsaturated fats and often used for frying and baking. Each oil serves different culinary and nutritional needs.</p></p>
