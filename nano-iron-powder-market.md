<p><ul><li>Spherical Nano Iron Powder</li><li>Flake Nano Iron Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano iron powder market is divided into two primary types: spherical and flake. Spherical nano iron powder features a round shape, enhancing its flow characteristics and reducing friction, making it ideal for applications like magnetic materials and electronics. In contrast, flake nano iron powder presents a flat, thin shape, which improves surface area and facilitates better dispersion in composites and coatings. Both types find applications in various industries, including electronics, automotive, and environmental remediation.</p></p>
