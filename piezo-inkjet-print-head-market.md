<p><p>A Piezo Inkjet Print Head is a sophisticated component used in inkjet printers that utilizes piezoelectric technology to eject ink droplets onto substrates. Unlike thermal inkjet heads, which use heat to create vapor bubbles, piezo print heads employ mechanical stress applied through piezoelectric materials to create precise ink ejections. This technology allows for greater control over droplet size and placement, leading to high-resolution prints and improved ink efficiency.</p><p>The Piezo Inkjet Print Head Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Piezo Inkjet Print Head market is divided into two main types: MEMS and Non-MEMS inkjet printheads. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) inkjet printheads utilize microfabrication techniques to produce tiny nozzles, offering precise droplet control and high resolution, ideal for applications requiring fine detail. In contrast, Non-MEMS inkjet printheads employ traditional manufacturing methods with larger nozzles, suitable for high-speed printing applications but may compromise on print resolution. Both types cater to diverse printing needs across various industries.</p></p>
