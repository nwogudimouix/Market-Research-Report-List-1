Cloud-based solutions store data on remote servers, enabling scalability, flexibility, and accessibility from any location. On-premise solutions, in contrast, involve software installed on local servers within a healthcare facility, providing greater control but requiring significant investment in infrastructure and maintenance. Each type caters to specific needs within the telemedicine landscape.</p></p>
