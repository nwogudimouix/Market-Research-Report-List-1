<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embolization Particle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microspheres</li><li>Particles (e.g. PVA Particles, Gelfoam Particles)</li><li>Drug-eluting Beads (DEBs)</li><li>Radio-Embolic Microspheres (e.g. Therasphere and SIRSphere)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embolization particle market includes various types used in interventional procedures. Microspheres are tiny spherical particles facilitating targeted blood flow obstruction. Particles like PVA and Gelfoam are widely employed for embolization due to their efficiency and ease of use. Drug-eluting beads (DEBs) combine embolization with localized drug delivery for enhanced therapeutic effects. Radio-embolic microspheres, such as Therasphere and SIRSphere, are infused with radioactive materials, offering precise tumor targeting and minimizing damage to surrounding tissues during cancer treatment.</p></p>
