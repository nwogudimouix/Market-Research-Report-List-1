<p><p>The Search and Rescue helmet market comprises several key types: ABS Protective Helmets, HDPE Protective Helmets, and FRP Protective Helmets. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) helmets are lightweight and durable, suitable for general rescue operations. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) helmets offer high impact resistance and are often used in harsher environments. FRP (Fiber Reinforced Plastic) helmets provide excellent strength-to-weight ratios and are designed for high-temperature situations, making them ideal for specialized rescue missions, ensuring safety and protection for rescuers.</p></p>
